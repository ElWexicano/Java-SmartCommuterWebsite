Public Transport App.

SmartCommuter is a public transport application for Android Smartphones. It Provides up to the minute information on public transport systems in Ireland.

About the app

The application will give the user control of their commuting experience by giving them more choice on how and when they use public transport. No longer will commuters be waiting and wondering when their bus will arrive or if they have enough time to go to the shop before the trains departs. No more waiting at the bus stop and looking at their watch wondering if the bus has already arrived and departed. With this application commuters can use their Android Smartphones to see exactly how long till the bus arrives and the current whereabouts of the bus or train.

It's Features

Commuters will be able to view information on the different transport stations, view timetables, view routes, view transportation modes on a map and allow them to plan their journey. Using the Smartphone’s built in GPS system SmartCommuter can provide information about nearby stations such as when next departure or arrival will happen and even provide directions to the station.

The Benefits

SmartCommuter aims at giving it's user the following benefits:

  • Greater flexibility in how you decide to commute.
  • Reduced uncertainty in Public Transport Systems.
  • Instant access to wide range of Public Transport Information Systems.
  • Ease of access to the some biggest Public Transport Systems in Ireland.
